The DataIn 1958, Billboard began weekly tracking of the top 100 songs in the United States, regardless of genre, by sales and radio play. When music transitioned to digital and streaming formats, Billboard also transitioned as well to include online sales and streams. Today, the Hot 100 is comprised mostly of the top 100 online streams of the week.

SpotifySpotify stores metadata for all of the songs in its music library.
Using Spotify we searched and found audio features for 24,526 of the 27,544 songs in our Hot 100 data set.
